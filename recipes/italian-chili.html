Italian-style Chili

Description

Che bello!

Chili with Italian sausage, roasted red peppers, and Marinara Sauce simmer together for a flavorful and hearty Italian style chili that will soon be a family favorite. Add fun toppings like parsley, grated cheese, and sliced cherry peppers to take this simple meal to the next level or substitute with your favorite plant-based protein for a delicious, high-protein vegan meal.

Satisy your buds (taste buds, that is), and those of loved ones with this Italian variation of a classic cold-weather dish.

Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil-Rich Paste
  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. hot Italian Sausage
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 jar (24 oz.) Marinara Sauce
  • 2 C Chicken Broth, low sodium
  • 2 cans (15 oz. each) cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 roasted red peppers, drained, chopped

Optional Toppings

  • grated parmasan
  • chopped Italian parsley (or basil)
  • giardiniera (drained, chopped)
  • cherry peppers (sliced)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot, on medium high heat; add meats; cook for 6-8 min. or until done; use a wooden spoon to break up large pieces
  2. Add onions and cook for 3-4 min. or until onions are translucent, stirring frequently. Stir in sauce, broth, beans, and roasted peppers. Cook for 7-10 min. or until heated clear through. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
  3. Sprinkle with optional toppings before serving.
  4. Variations - Substitute 2-3 Italian Turkey sausage links with the casings removed for the bulk Italian sausage. Make this chili vegan by substituting plant-based Italian sausage & vegan protein crumbles and vegetable broth.
